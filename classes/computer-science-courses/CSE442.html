CSE 442 Data Visualization

The artifact above is the final project my group created for Data Visualization (see website).

This course and project really helped me see the benefit of having people with different areas of expertise. This elective was completely project based, with the majority of the work being done in groups. One of the moments that I can distinctly remember the most was how I did not know how to process large amounts of data in CSV format, and one of my group members wrote a solution in two lines using a python tool I was not familiar with. Meanwhile, I had a lot of NodeJS experience and was able to heavily contribute with the infrastructure of the project. This was a great learning experience for me, as I felt that the work I did on that team was a great example of Adam Smith and the division of labor :)
