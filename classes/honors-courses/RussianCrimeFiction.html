Russian Crime Fiction

The above artifact is the final paper I wrote for my Russian Crime Fiction course.

What this artifact represents to me is my continued interest in literature. Although I have been taking a lot of technical courses and extra curriculars, I have always been fascinated by reading. Additionally, I think that there is something inherently human about trying to manifest one's thoughts through essays or discussions, and this was a class in which I felt I was able to continue to develop those skills. Professor Galya Diment did a great job of facilitating an intellectually engaging environment, despite the fact that this course was online. I especially loved the format she gave for the midterm and final, as her questions left room for a lot of creative expression. I am especially proud of the final that is pictured above, as I thought that I was able to communicate fairly complex ideas in an interesting yet digestable way.
