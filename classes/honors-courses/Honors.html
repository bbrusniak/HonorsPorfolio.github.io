History of the Aztecs

The idea of the "Aztecs" is deeply embedded into the minds of many different people. Perhaps it conjures up images of cannibals or the mythistory of Hernan Cortes. This course focused on who the Mexica were and how biased sources can be used to still draw convincing conclusions.

Near/Middle East Illustrated

Through a variety of reading material, with a focus on a set of graphic novels, “(Near)/Middle East Illustrated” discusses verbal and visual expressions in order to represent Near and Middle East.

Russian Crime Fiction

Introduces important trends and movements in Russian literary and cultural history through the reading and analysis of various crime fiction novels ranging from Dostoevsky's Crime and Punishment to Boris Akunin's The Winter Queen
